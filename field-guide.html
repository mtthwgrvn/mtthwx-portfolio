The Innovation Research Field Guide

Plays, Methods & Outputs.

A practical guide to turning opportunistic, one-off research into a scalable practice — sixty-three field-tested plays, the systems that hold them, and the process to ship them.

Browse the plays See the framework

01 — The Shift

Research is not a cost center that ends with a report. It is a practice — a repeatable engine that turns each study into reusable assets, sharper proposals, and durable credibility.

02 — The Three Pillars

What you build so the work can scale.

Pillar i

Systems

  • ResearchOpsTemplates for briefs, protocols, and lightweight ethics checklists that manage the study pipeline.
  • Knowledge managementA central, tagged research library — findings you can retrieve and reuse, not scatter.
  • Data infrastructureStandardized sources and documented provenance; automate the pulls you repeat.
  • Marketing integrationA publication calendar that converts outputs into business-development assets.

Pillar ii

Tools

  • Modeling & data sciencePython, spatial analysis, life-cycle and techno-economic modeling, predictive models.
  • Version controlPrivate repos for research artifacts and code; reproducibility by default.
  • AutomationScripts and API connections that cut manual data wrangling.
  • PublishingDesign and BI tooling for client-facing reports, dashboards, and infographics.

Pillar iii

Processes

  • Proposal workflowA boilerplate that makes scoping documents fast and consistent.
  • Research sprintsTime-boxed cycles that give delivery a predictable cadence.
  • Peer review & QAAn internal checklist that keeps quality and consistency high.
  • Post-project debriefsCaptured lessons that compound into institutional knowledge.

03 — Core Methods

The technical range behind the plays.

M1

Data science & statistics

Regression and time-series forecasting; machine learning for optimization and segmentation.

M2

Life-cycle & techno-economic

Impact assessment, life-cycle costing, systems modeling, levelized cost and cost-of-ownership.

M3

GIS & spatial analysis

Site suitability, equity overlays, and spatial clustering across a service territory.

M4

Human-systems & UX

Usability and human-in-the-loop testing — from interfaces to cyber-physical systems.

M5

Knowledge management

Information architecture, taxonomies, schemas, and metadata strategy.

M6

Speculative & scenario

Futures and foresight — the move that turns an assessment into a prospective one.

07 — The Roadmap

Four moves from zero to a practice.

Phase 0

Establish

Name the practice, secure sign-off, and stand up the lightest possible version.

Phase 1

Build foundations

Launch two pilots from the plays; build the initial tooling and libraries.

Phase 2

Systematize

Finish the pilots, document the playbooks, and prep findings for reuse.

Phase 3

Scale & monetize

Automate the workflow and turn outputs into a repeatable revenue line.
